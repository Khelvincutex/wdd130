Overview

Purpose

As the proud owner of our newly established rafting business, our primary purpose of establishing an online presence is to augment our reach to a wider audience, display our services, and attract potential customers. With a website, social media accounts, and online booking options, we will make it easier for people to find and choose our services. Additionally, our online platform will serve as an information hub, offering detailed itineraries, safety guidelines, and frequently asked questions, ensuring that our customers are well-informed and prepared for their adventure.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website comprises adventure devotees, outdoor seekers, and adrenaline junkies who are eager to experience the excitement of water rafting. The website is designed to cater to individuals and groups looking for unique and memorable outdoor activities.

Scenario Questions:

  1. Question: Are you an adventure devotee seeking thrilling outdoor experiences?

    2. Answer: Our water rafting adventures are tailored for individuals who crave excitement and are passionate about exploring the great outdoors.

  2. Question: What sets our rafting companies apart from other rafting companies?

    3. Answer: We offer a unique blend of top notch saftey measure, and experienced guides who ensure an unforgettable journey down the river.

  3. Question: Do you want a user-friendly platform for easy online booking and comprehensive information about rafting itineraries and safety measures?

    4. Answer: Our website provides a seamless online booking experience and serves as an information hub, offering detailed itineraries and safety guidelines to ensure you are well-prepared for your rafting adventure.
